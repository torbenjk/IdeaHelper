IdeaHelper

The IdeaHelper is a simple tool, that helps you to come up with ideas. It works like this:

  1. First you describe your challenge in a concrete way
  2. Then you are prompted several times with different words and images, and asked to think about how these helps you come up with solutions to your challenge
  3. Finally, you get the full list of all the ideas you came up with
